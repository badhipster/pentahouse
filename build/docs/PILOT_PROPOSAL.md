## What we do

A 5-agent system that catches every inquiry in <60 seconds, scores it, drafts the WhatsApp reply for your rep to approve, tracks the visit, and traces every booking back to the ad rupee that bought it.

**Your existing CRM keeps working.** We sit on top, do the work it records but never finishes.

## The pilot — 4 weeks, ₹50,000 flat

### What we install on your floor

- Real-time inquiry capture from Meta + Google + 99acres + Housing.com + MagicBricks
- AI-drafted WhatsApp replies in English / Hindi / Marathi, **approved by your team before they send**
- A Newsroom-of-Sales dashboard showing every action across your team and the AI assistants
- Post-visit objection extraction with deal-breaker / negotiation / minor severity flags
- Full booking attribution: ad source → inquiry → score → visit → booking

### What we require from you

- One sales head as the primary user (you)
- 2 reps minimum on the floor connected to the system
- Read-only access to your existing lead sources (or your team forwards CSV exports daily)
- 30 minutes for kickoff, 30 minutes for week-2 check-in, 30 minutes for week-4 review

### Success criteria — both required

1. **Median first-response time under 60 seconds** across all inbound channels
2. **At least 30 inquiries qualified per day** across the floor

**If we miss either metric: full refund. No questions.**

We do not negotiate the success criteria. They are objective, measurable from the dashboard, and we set the floor at a level we are confident we can clear.

### What "qualified" means

A lead that has either:
- Captured 3+ intent fields (purpose, budget, timeline, config, city — any 3)
- OR responded once to our Nurture Agent's WhatsApp draft

This is the same definition our internal eval set uses. You see it live in the dashboard.
